记录一次跟shopify客户的沟通记录

发表于2022-10-27|更新于2022-10-27
|阅读量:

shopify回答关于能不能卖翻新机

Me :Hello, I want to inquire about the sales of refurbished iPhones, Xiaomi, OPPO, vivo and other mobile phones on shopify. Are there any requirements?

Ian:Hi. My name is Ian with the Retail Support team here at Shopify. Thank you for your patience.

Would the sale of these items be done online or through a physical retail location?

Me:only online

Are there any requirements?

lan:I’l be up-front about this as I hope you’d expect that Shopify would be - we are unable to provide advice to our merchants as to what they can or cannot sell. The answers to such questions can be highly nuanced and context-specific with a complex legal landscape.

I can provide some very general guidance:

All Shopify merchants must comply with Shopify’s Terms of Service and Acceptable Use Policy (AUP). This includes complying with all applicable laws in the merchant’s jurisdiction and their customers’ jurisdiction(s).

It is often nuanced and context-specific to determine whether certain products and services comply with the laws applicable in a merchant’s jurisdiction, as well as your customers’ jurisdiction(s). For example, some products may be legal in one place, but illegal if shipped to another place. It’s impossible for Shopify to understand all of the context relevant to the merchant’s products, the customer’s jurisdiction, and the laws of every country where merchants and customers live.

For this reason, the legality of products is something that you will need to sort out outside of Shopify.

If you are using, or are intending to use, Shopify Payments you must also comply with the relevant Shopify Payments Terms of Service applicable to your jurisdiction.

As such, We suggest that you seek independent legal advice for questions about:

  1. Your compliance with Shopify’s terms and/or policies; or,
  2. The legality of products or services in your jurisdiction and/or their customers’ jurisdiction(s).
